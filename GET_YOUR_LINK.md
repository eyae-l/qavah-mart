# How to Get Your Deployment Link

## Method 1: Vercel Dashboard (Easiest)

1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click on your project (Qavah-mart)
3. You'll see your deployment URL at the top
4. It looks like: `https://qavah-mart.vercel.app` or `https://your-project-name.vercel.app`

## Method 2: After Pushing to GitHub

After you run `git push origin main`, Vercel will:
1. Automatically start building
2. Send you an email when done
3. The email contains your deployment link

## Method 3: Check Vercel CLI (if installed)

```bash
vercel ls
```

This shows all your deployments and their URLs.

## Your Link Will Look Like

One of these formats:
- `https://qavah-mart.vercel.app` (production)
- `https://qavah-mart-git-main-yourname.vercel.app` (branch)
- `https://qavah-mart-abc123.vercel.app` (preview)

## The Link Works On

✅ Desktop computers
✅ Laptops
✅ Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
✅ Mobile phones (iPhone, Android)
✅ Any device with a web browser
✅ Anywhere in the world

## Share Your Link

Once you have it, you can:
- Share it with anyone
- Open it on your phone
- Send it to friends/family
- Post it on social media
- Use it for testing

## Example

If your project is called "qavah-mart", your link is probably:
```
https://qavah-mart.vercel.app
```

Just replace "qavah-mart" with your actual project name.

## Quick Steps to Get Link Right Now

1. Open browser
2. Go to: https://vercel.com
3. Sign in
4. Click your project
5. Copy the URL shown at the top

That's your link! It works on every device.
